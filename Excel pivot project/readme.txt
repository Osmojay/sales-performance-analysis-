Executive Summary – Jumia Sales Analysis Dashboard

This project provides a comprehensive analysis of Jumia’s sales performance, 
highlighting key revenue drivers, customer behavior, 
and temporal trends. The dashboard summarizes 1,000 total orders generating $3.52M in revenue, 
with an average customer spend of $3,520.98 and an average 11.95 days between orders, indicating strong 
customer value and repeat purchase potential.

Revenue analysis shows that Anniversary, Raksha Bandhan, and Holi are the
 strongest occasions, confirming the importance of seasonal 
and event-based campaigns. By category, Colors, Soft Toys, and Sweets contribute the highest revenue,
 while product-level insights reveal Magram Set and Quia Gift as top-performing items.

Time-based trends indicate higher order values during midday to evening hours, supporting targeted promotion timing.
 Monthly performance peaks around February and August, aligning with major occasions. City-level analysis 
highlights Imphal, Dhanbad, and Kavali as top order contributors, offering clear direction for regional marketing focus.

Overall, this dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by 
identifying high-impact products, occasions,
 and customer segments, supporting optimized promotions, inventory planning, and revenue growth strategies.
